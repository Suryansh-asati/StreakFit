Our Story – The Spark Behind StreakFit

StreakFit was born out of a simple realization:
Most people don’t struggle to start working out — they struggle to stay consistent.

As developers and students trying to build better habits ourselves, we noticed how often we’d lose momentum after just a few days. Gym plans, fitness trackers, and step goals weren’t enough — we needed a daily push and a way to stay accountable. That’s when the idea clicked: What if fitness felt more like a game? What if consistency was visual, rewarding, and even competitive? So, we created StreakFit — a lightweight, streak-based fitness tracker that rewards progress and makes it fun to show up every day. It's not about doing more. It’s about not stopping. We built it for ourselves — and now, we’re building it for everyone.
