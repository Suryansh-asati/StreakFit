Our Story – The Spark Behind StreakFit

StreakFit was born out of a simple realization:
Most people don’t struggle to start working out, they struggle to stay consistent.

Like many, we struggled to stay consistent with fitness beyond the first few days. Step goals and workout plans weren’t enough, we needed daily motivation and accountability. That’s when the idea clicked: What if fitness felt fun, visual, and motivating like a game? StreakFit was our answer, a simple, streak-based fitness app that makes progress rewarding and consistent. We built it to help ourselves, and now, we’re sharing it with everyone.
